This website will provide up-to-date information about various trains, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station.</p>

</ul>
<h3><a id="user-content-instructions" class="anchor" href="#instructions" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Instructions</h3>
<ul>
<li>Make sure that your app suits this basic spec:
<ul>
<li>When adding trains, administrators should be able to submit the following:
<ul>
<li>Train Name</li>
<li>Destination</li>
<li>First Train Time -- in military time</li>
<li>Frequency -- in minutes</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Code this app to calculate when the next train will arrive; this should be relative to the current time.</li>
<li>Users from many different machines must be able to view same train times.</li>
<li>Styling and theme are completely up to you. Get Creative!</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
